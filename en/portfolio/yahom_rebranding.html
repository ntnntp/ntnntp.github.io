In this course, students will be trained to use various graphic designing programs, especially Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop. We were given legacy product with outdated designs and assigned to redesign (or even rebrand if prefer) the product.

The product our group got was "Baby In The Golden Tray Herbal Medicine" (which will be called Yahom as it more convenient.) For this project, we flattened the logo, eliminating black borders and recolored elements of the logo design. We even decided to go overboard and create 3D render of the following design. The packaging form is even reconsidered too: being a jar to a sachet, to make dosage more accurate and easier to keep.
