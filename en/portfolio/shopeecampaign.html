Shopee 9.9 Contest 2020: Shopee New Normal

SHOPEE, 2020

AWARDED
GRAPHIC DESIGN
ILLUSTRATOR
PHOTOSHOP
MARKETING
Submitted: September 2020
Role: Graphic Designer, UX Analyst 
Awarded: Honorary Mentions (National)
The contest is mainly about designing marketing campaign for the Shopee 9.9 shopping spree in September of every year. We are challenged by four demographic sectors; however, we opted for elderly as for the consideration of wealth.

After the announcement of prizes, we got a chance to talk with Mr. Piti Laulathaphol, Head of Online Marketing at Shopee about our projects and received many valuable feedbacks. For my own development, I asked Mr. Piti about the thing that I can improve about my design; and he pointed out some major considerations and also teaches me about the concept of designing advertising posters as a plus.

Overall, it was a fantastic experience and I am very proud to end up in the top 8 out of thousands teams for nationwide contest like this one.
