Similar to Shopee contest, this contest is mainly about designing marketing plans with the problem statement of how to increase Shopback users and increase the credibility of an application in challenging demographic sectors.

We chose to opt for the university student sectors in this contest and did an extensive analysis, and some user experience research to find out what can we help the user to achieve their goals.

Overall, this is surely another great experience and I, again, am also very proud to end up in top 6 teams across the nation.
